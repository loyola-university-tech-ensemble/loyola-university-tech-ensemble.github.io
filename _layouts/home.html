What is LUTE?

We are a contemporary “laptop orchestra”, but our broader title encapsulates our music technology research and classic ensemble instrumentation.

We actively perform on and off campus, check out our upcoming performances below.

Our Tech

Our In C app enables the performance of Terry Riley's minimalist standard In C regardless of the musical experience. It is currently being developed into a web app.

The app also pairs well with the Progress Viewer, designed to visualize the current progress of an In C performance typically with a projector.

Still curious? All of our software projects are available here.
